About Me

I started learning to code in 2019, by way of a coding bootcamp, which I completed in 2020. Because of the unemployment rate during Covid, finding a web developer position out of bootcamp proved to be an impossible task. Nonetheless, I kept at it after, honing my craft. In 2021, I had a series of family tragedies: my mom broke her hip and my dad and my grandpa passed away within a month of each other, which gave me no choice but to take care of my family after that, taking a hiatus from coding until recently. Here are some of my hobbies, outside of coding:

  • Bicycling
  • Rowing
  • Watching sports
  • Playing banjo, violin, and drums
  • Pickleball
  • Ceramics

Tech has been something I have always shared a passion for and in retrospect, I wish I had pursued it as more than a hobby earlier in my life rather than making the mistakes that I have ended up making. But better late than never, I suppose. Where I lack in experience, I make up for in passion, motivation, and problem solving skills.
