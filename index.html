About Me

I started learning to code in 2019, by way of a coding bootcamp, which I completed in 2020. Because of the unemployment rate during Covid, finding a web developer position out of bootcamp proved to be an far more difficult task, coupled with some issues that forced me to take a step back. Nonetheless, I have been keeping at it since, honing my craft. Here are some of my hobbies, outside of coding:

  • Bicycling
  • Rowing
  • Watching sports. In my free time, I am also a contributor for American Scouser, a blog that focuses on Liverpool FC
  • Playing banjo, violin, and drums
  • Pickleball
  • Ceramics

But the question is, why should you hire me? Tech has been something I have always shared a passion for. Where I lack in experience, I make up for in passion, motivation, and problem solving skills. Plus it's all about helping people and organizations to improve.
